Finding the Parking Garages

Use the map below to navigate to the WSCC Main Garage or the Freeway Park Garage. Upon entry, be sure to keep your ticket with you rather than in your vehicle.

Exiting the Parking Garages

Take a parking ticket as you enter the garage. Keep this ticket with you when you leave your vehicle.

Prior to returning to your vehicle, insert your ticket in one of the three automated pay stations or at the central cashier kiosk conveniently located at the main garage parking entrance on Level 3.  An additional, automated station is located just inside the garage on the 8th Avenue entrance/exit in Aisle A, by the crosswalk.

You can use cash or major credit cards to pay the parking fee. Upon payment, the automated pay stations or cashier will validate your ticket and return it to you.

Insert your validated ticket in the machine at the exit gate as you leave the garage, with the arrow and number side of ticket face up and pointing towards the machine. If additional assistance is needed please press the intercom button.

Green Parking

Electric Vehicle Parking: Four EV charging stations are available in the WSCC Main Garage (Aisle A) for current guest access. These stations can charge all new generation electric and plug-in hybrid vehicles. Charging is free for the first two hours, and $2 per hour at the start of each hour after that. Regular parking rates apply. 

Bicycle Parking: The WSCC Main Garage offers parking for 10-15 bikes on the Blue Level, just next to the Electric Vehicle Parking. We also have various locking bike stands on Convention Place, 7th and Pike, and on Pike Street in front of the Center. Bike storage is not available at the WSCC.

Disabled Parking

WSCC Main Garage: There are 16 disabled parking spaces located at the end of Aisle F on the Blue level. If you are parking in one of the disabled spaces you can use the adjacent blue ramp to enter WSCC. There is no elevator access inside the WSCC Main Garage. As you enter WSCC from the parking structure you will be on level 3. An accessible elevator is on your right-hand side, adjacent to Room 310.

Freeway Park Garage: There are 16 disabled spaces available, four spaces on each of the four levels, all located near the elevators. If you park in the disabled spaces in Freeway Park Garage and need wheelchair accessibility, take the elevator to the PARK Level. As you exit the elevator you will turn to the right and proceed across Freeway Park Plaza to the Level 4 entrance of Washington State Convention Center. Please note that Freeway Park Plaza is an uncovered area open to the elements.

Validation

Meeting Planners can arrange to use a validation ticket to pay for their attendee's parking. Arrangements for validation must be made with the Event Manager thirty days before the event. For short-term bookings, exception will be made. Parking validation does not provide in and out privileges or guarantee space availability. At the close of the event, unused validation tickets will be returned and the Meeting Planner will be billed for validations collected as part of the event settlement invoice.

For additional parking information, contact us at parking@wscc.com
