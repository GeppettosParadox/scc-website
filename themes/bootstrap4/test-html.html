From wide-open spaces and breathtaking views, to warm and cozy seating areas, we have the perfect venue for what you have planned. Whether it’s an intimate gathering or a large convention, your attendees will have an unforgettable experience at the Northwest’s premier meeting and event facility. We offer renowned quality of service, award winning cuisine, state of the art technology and exceptional interior design finishes, all in the heart of beautiful Seattle.

SUMMIT BUILDING: OPENING 2022

Located in the heart of downtown Seattle just one block northeast of our existing facility, the Washington State Convention Center’s Summit building will double the capacity of the Center’s offerings and is scheduled to open spring 2022.

FROM OUR PRESIDENT & CEO

We are excited to share the news that construction has officially begun on the Summit building, our additional facility in downtown Seattle. Just one block from our existing facility (Arch), Summit will more than double our current capacity.

Meeting planners: sales activities will begin mid-August coinciding with the ASAE Annual Meeting in Chicago. Please contact me or any member of our Sales team if you have any questions.

Neighbors, vendors, and community members: you can find updates on the early construction activities or pursue vendor opportunities at  www.wsccaddition.com.

Stay tuned!

Jeffrey A. Blosser
President & CEO
