Contact & Staff

Washington State Convention Center's staff includes event specialists with many years of experience planning and directing meetings and events of all sizes. We welcome any questions you have about event planning services, our facility, our amenities and technology, our catering and on-site dining, and more. From the largest event to a more intimate gathering, please let us know how we can best assist you.
